Vampire Fight

Rules:

  • Bite wins vs Scratch and will gain you 1 HP.
  • Scratch wins vs Garlic and will gain you 1 HP.
  • Garlic wins vs Bite and will gain you 1 HP.
  • Silver Bullet will take away 3 HP from the opponent, but you only have 2 shots.
  • Stake will take away 2 HP from the opponent, but you only have 2
  • Crossbow does 4 damage, but you only have 1 shot.

Player 1 and Player 2, choose your names.
